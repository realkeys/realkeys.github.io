RODO'S: LEGACY is a FNaF horror game created by REALKEYS. The game is an unofficial expansion of the Three Nights at Rodo's game series by RockonFnaf.
Set in 1956, the game follows the player, a young teenager, who breaks into the old Rodo's Museum, which is now abandoned following its company's closure in 1932.
The power turns on after the player gets to the night office, and they get trapped inside. Can they find a way out?/div>
