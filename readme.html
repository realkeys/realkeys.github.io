My Games

Coming Home is a psychological horror game. Thomas Williams awakens in a hospital with no memory of how, when, or why he got there.

It's All a Waste is a episodic survival horror game. Droid Toys, Inc. closes down, and reporter Sam Willbanks breaks into the abandoned facility to write a story on why.
